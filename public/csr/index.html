What to Donate?




Unused Clothes

You might have clothes that you have not used for ages. It's time you let those clothes help someone.

Blankets

Extra blankets that we throw now can provide warm sleep to kids affected by flood.

Medicines

Medicines for headache, wound, pain and dehydration can help a lot.




Sanitary Items

Toothpaste, soap, shampoo, sanitary pads can help people remain healthy in flood affected areas.

Food

Anything that can kill hunger and is healthy will be a great contribution from you at this time of crisis.

Love, Wishes and Shares

We sometimes can not contribute inspite of having strong willingness. In that case, share and ask others to contribute.
